## Prerequisites

Before starting, make sure you have:

<Check>
  An Integrabot account (if not, [create one here](https://app.integrabot.ai/register))
</Check>
<Check>
  A phone number for WhatsApp Business
</Check>
<Check>
  Basic information about your business (products/services)
</Check>

## Step 1: Connect your WhatsApp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Panel">
    Log in at [app.integrabot.ai](https://app.integrabot.ai) and click **WhatsApp** in the sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Connect New Channel">
    On the WhatsApp screen, click **"+ Connect Channel"**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scan the QR Code">
    - Open WhatsApp on your phone
    - Go to **Settings > Linked Devices**
    - Tap **Link a Device**
    - Scan the QR Code displayed on screen
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Connection">
    After scanning, wait a few seconds. You'll see a success message and your channel will be connected!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Important:** Use an exclusive number for the bot. Don't use the same number on other devices simultaneously.
</Warning>

## Step 2: Configure your AI Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access My Agent">
    In the sidebar, click **My Agent**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Define the Goal">
    In the **Main Goal** field, describe your agent's purpose
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Write Instructions">
    In the **Instructions** field, detail how the agent should behave
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Communication Style">
    Select communication temperature:
    - **0.3** (Balanced) - Recommended to start
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    Click **Save** at the top of the page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can test your agent by clicking the chat icon in the bottom right corner of the screen!
</Info>

## Step 3: Add Knowledge

For your agent to respond accurately, it needs to know your business.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Direct Text (Fastest)">
    1. Go to **Knowledge Base** in menu
    2. Click **Text** tab
    3. Paste important information
    4. Click **Save**
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Import Website">
    1. Go to **Knowledge Base**
    2. Click **URLs** tab
    3. Paste your site link
    4. Click **Import**
    5. Wait for processing
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Upload Document">
    1. Go to **Knowledge Base**
    2. Click **Documents** tab
    3. Drag PDF, DOC or TXT files
    4. Wait for upload and processing
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 4: Test your Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Test Chat">
    Click the **chat** icon in the bottom right corner
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Simulate a Conversation">
    Ask questions as if you were a customer
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Evaluate Responses">
    - Are responses correct?
    - Is the tone appropriate?
    - Is the agent using knowledge base information?
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust if Necessary">
    If something isn't good, go back to **My Agent** and adjust instructions
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations! Your agent is now active and ready to serve.

### What happens now?

1. Anyone who sends a message to your WhatsApp number will be served by the agent
2. The agent will use the instructions and knowledge you configured
3. You can monitor all conversations in real-time in the **Conversations** menu
4. If needed, you can intervene manually in any conversation
